  • what is Hands on the Farm

    Welcome to Hands on the Farm, an interactive game designed to get you moving while having fun! Help take care of Finn by catching chickens as they try to escape from their coop. Use your hands to grab regular chickens and hit the ones wearing helmets. Activate your ultimate ability by jumping with both feet on the floor sensors. Explore the entire farm alongside Finn through multiple levels.

  • How do I play?

    Hands on the Farm uses a camera, a button, and a floor pad. The camera tracks your hand movements as you grab the chickens to feed your favorite fox. With a single press, the button allows you to make tougher chickens catchable by striking them. The floor pad lets you activate your ultimate ability by jumping on it with both feet. Your ultimate ability stuns all chickens on screen for 5 seconds.

    Each level is made up of multiple waves that gradually increase in difficulty. Throughout these waves, you will encounter different types of chickens. Your mission is to catch them and feed Finn. Watch out! If a chicken remains on screen for more than 15 seconds without being caught, it will escape permanently. When this happens, you lose one of your five lives. All lives are restored at the start of each new wave.
